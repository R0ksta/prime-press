Service Policy

1. Introduction

At Pandey Press Ironing Service, we are committed to providing high-quality ironing services to ensure your garments are handled with care and returned to you in the best possible condition. This policy outlines the terms and conditions governing our garment care services.

2. Pricing & Payment Terms

General Pricing: Prices are based on garment type, fabric, and care instructions. A detailed price list can be found on our website. All services are priced according to the type of garment and level of service. Additional fees may apply for difficult items, special treatments, or expedited services.

Promotions: Discounts or promotions may apply and will be clearly communicated at the time of service.

Minimum Charge: A minimum charge of $30.00 applies to all orders.

3. Customer Responsibilities

3.1 Garment Condition

  • Customers should ensure that garments are free of any hazardous materials (e.g., pins, coins, or other items) before drop-off.
  • Customers should inform us of any specific instructions related to fabric care, size, or fit.
  • Items must be clean and fully dry. We reserve the right to refuse soiled or damp items.

3.2 Garment Tags

Items should be accompanied by their care tags, or customers should inform us of any special instructions if the care tag is missing.

3.3 Unclaimed Items

Garments must be picked up within 30 days from the completion date. After this period, unclaimed items may incur a storage fee of $30, or may be donated or disposed of at our discretion.

4. Refund & Dispute Resolution

4.1 Refund Policy

If a customer is unsatisfied with the service, we will attempt to resolve the issue by offering a re-press, repair, or refund in cases of significant service failure. Refund requests must be made within 5 days of item pickup.

Where a refund or replacement is applicable, Pandey Press will determine the refund percentage based on the condition and age of the garment. Depreciation will be considered. Pandey Press will request proof of purchase when and where possible.

4.2 Damage Claims

If a customer believes their garment has been damaged due to our service, they must report the issue within 5 days of pickup. We will assess the damage and, if determined to be our fault, compensate the customer based on the value of the garment or provide a replacement. Customers should provide proof where possible, including photographs and proof of purchase.

5. Liability

While we take utmost care to ensure the safety and quality of your garments, Pandey Press Ironing Service will not be held responsible for any damage or loss caused by factors beyond our control, such as:

  • Natural wear and tear.
  • Fabric weaknesses or defects.
  • Customer-provided garment instructions or incorrect labeling.
  • Handover: Customer is responsible for adequate handover of clothing. Items must be bagged or in basket/s; clothes should not exceed the capacity of the basket and should not overflow.
  • Loss Claims: If a customer is claiming loss of an item, they should provide as much information as possible (description, brand, size, color, etc.).
