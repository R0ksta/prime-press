Service Policy

1. Minimum Charge

To ensure the quality of our service, a minimum charge of $30.00 applies to all ironing orders. If the total cost of your items is less than $30.00, the minimum fee will be billed.

2. Drop-Off & Collection

We operate strictly on a drop-off and pick-up basis from our location in Blackburn, VIC. We do not currently offer a mobile collection or delivery service.

Please wait for an email confirmation before arriving for drop-off or collection.

3. Hangers & Packaging

Customers are encouraged to provide their own hangers. If hangers are not provided, items will be neatly folded. We do not provide plastic garment covers in an effort to reduce waste.

4. Payment

Payment is required in Cash upon drop-off of your garments. Electronic payments are not currently accepted.

5. Garment Care

Items must be provided clean and dry. We reserve the right to refuse items that are soiled, damp, or require specialist dry cleaning. While we take every precaution, Pandey Press is not liable for damage to buttons, zippers, or delicate fabrics that are not suited for industrial steam ironing.
