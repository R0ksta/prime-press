FAQs

Everything you need to know about our professional ironing service.

General Information

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Blackburn, VIC 3130, and primarily serve the local community and surrounding suburbs within the Whitehorse area.

What are your operating hours?

Mon - Fri: 17:00 – 21:00
Sat - Sun: 09:00 – 17:00
All drop-offs and pickups are strictly by appointment.

Preparation & Hangers

Do I need to provide my own hangers?

Yes. If you require your items to be returned on hangers, please provide them at drop-off. Otherwise, items will be returned neatly folded.

How should I prepare my clothes?

Garments must be clean and fully dry. Please ensure all pockets are emptied of coins or sharp objects.

Pricing & Payment

Is there a minimum order amount?

Yes, a minimum charge of $30.00 applies to all orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash or Bank Transfer only. Payment is due upon collection.
